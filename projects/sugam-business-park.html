Find Your Space at Sugam Business Park!

Located in the energetic Sector V, in the midst of the corporate action with the scenic view of North Kolkata wetlands is Sugam Homes commercial building; Sugam Business Park. It’s the ideal workplace befitting grand ambitions.

The ten-storey, centrally air conditioned, furnished building offers spacious office spaces with a majestic 4-meter floor to ceiling height and column-less floors, which are fit to be used by corporate houses, startups, IT offices or financial institutes.

Along with a convenient location the project has been seamlessly designed with environment friendly offices and a futuristic mindset.

Developed and conceptualised by Sugam Homes, the space comes with the expertise of 3 decade experience and is made for success.
